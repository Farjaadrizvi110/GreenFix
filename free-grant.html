Free First Time Central Heating Grants

If you are living in a home without any heating system or one that doesn’t involve a boiler and radiators, then it’s important to know that you may qualify for the free first-time central heating grants. These central heating grants are part of the ECO4 scheme introduced by the government to tackle the issue of fuel poverty. By providing insulation to homes and promoting energy-efficient appliances, the country’s carbon footprint will significantly reduce. Such an initiative will contribute positively towards the environment, playing a significant role in reducing global warming.

We understand that the importance of a central heating system cannot be overemphasised, especially during the chilly winter months. By providing free central heating grants, the government aims to help households that are struggling to afford the cost of heating their homes. This scheme has made available various free grant services which you can see below:

FAQ's

My Income is just above £30,000, can I still apply?

Although you don’t meet the criteria for green home grants, however, you can still apply. You might be able to access other schemes that may become beneficial for you.

I live in social housing or a privately rented property, am I eligible?
Unfortunately, you may never be eligible for scheme 1 of green home grants, however, you are still eligible for a sustainable warmth scheme. You can apply for the sustainable warmth scheme to get grants.
What do I get from this scheme?
If you apply for this scheme under GreenFix Ltd, you’ll be able to get the following benefits.
  1. Save money due to grants offered by the government.
  2. Quality insulation that brings energy efficiency to your home.
  3. Controls your heating that reduces energy waste and carbon emissions.
  4. Offer helps and support.
